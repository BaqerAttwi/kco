What We Do

Our Services

Full-spectrum maintenance solutions for residential buildings, commercial properties, and corporate facilities throughout Beirut.

01
Building Maintenance
Full preventive and corrective maintenance for residential and commercial structures. Façades, lobbies, stairwells, rooftops, and more.
02
Electrical Works
Installation, repair, and inspection of electrical systems. Panel upgrades, wiring, lighting, generators and UPS maintenance.
03
Plumbing & Sanitation
Complete plumbing solutions: leak detection, pipe replacement, drainage systems, water tanks cleaning and pump maintenance.
04
HVAC Systems
Air conditioning installation, servicing and repairs. Central HVAC systems, splits, VRV, and ventilation maintenance contracts.
05
Elevator & Lift Services
Elevator maintenance contracts, inspection, modernisation and 24-hour breakdown response for all makes and models.
06
Renovation & Fit-Out
Interior and exterior renovation, painting, tiling, waterproofing, and full fit-out for commercial spaces and residential units.
07
Preventive Contracts
Custom annual maintenance contracts with scheduled visits, priority response, and full reporting — peace of mind, year-round.
08
Emergency Response
Rapid deployment for urgent repairs. Water damage, electrical faults, structural issues — we respond when it matters most.
